v2 Changes

Font: Switched to Cormorant Garamond Light — more refined and editorial than Instrument Serif, with elegant thin strokes that match the layered veil concept. Lighter weight feels more premium.

Mark size: Scaled the V up significantly so it reads clearly at small sizes and feels more balanced next to the wordmark.

Clean apex: Removed the dot at the bottom. The V now tapers to a clean, sharp point — more decisive, less cluttered.
